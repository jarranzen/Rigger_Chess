Completion Standard
vs
Tie + untie + hank: Complete the tie, fully untie everything, then re-hank all rope neatly. The full cycle.

Full tie + untie: Complete the tie then fully remove all rope from the column/body. No hank required.

Tie to completion: Finish the tie with proper lock-off or finishing knot. Standard completion.

Wraps only: Complete the wraps and structure but skip the final lock-off. For newer riggers building wrap speed and rope handling.
